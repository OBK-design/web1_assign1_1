About me

I attended an Art academy and spent the last five years working with Agencies, Light and Music Festivals, NGO's, and Startups.
I'm self-taught at heart, and I came to this program to give that raw experience structure to sculpt it into something deliberate.
If my past was the raw material, this program is the process of turning it into the work itself.

Coming to the IMD program for me is a personality match, the natural next step toward structured learning and a formal credential to complement work I had already been doing in the field.
I've often been told I'm a jack of all trades, master of none, but most people forget to finish the full quote: "but oftentimes better than master of one."
The IMD program offers exactly that kind of breadth, and that's what drew me to it. It aligns with who I am and where I want to go : “Interactive installation".

"Jack of all trades, master of none, but oftentimes better than master of one." Geffray Mynshul in 1618 & Charles Lucas in 1785
